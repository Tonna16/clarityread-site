ClarityRead — Reading Mode & Dyslexia Support

Toggle dyslexia-friendly fonts, reflow text, contrast, and read-aloud — all locally in your browser.

Read the web more comfortably — simple, private, and fast.

ClarityRead makes long articles and web pages easier to read by applying dyslexia-friendly fonts, reflowing text for narrow screens, offering high-contrast mode, and built-in read-aloud using your browser’s voices. Nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly opt in.

Key features

Dyslexia font
Switch to OpenDyslexic-style font for easier character recognition.
Reflow & reader
Reformats long articles into a readable column with adjustable font sizes.
Read-aloud
Uses your browser's speech synthesis — no text is sent externally by default.
High contrast
Toggle high-contrast and invert modes for better visibility.
